 Enterprise Sales & Profitability Intelligence Dashboard
 
📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes enterprise sales performance to uncover revenue trends, profit drivers, and margin erosion factors.
The objective was to understand why revenue growth does not always translate into profit growth.

🎯 Business Questions
Which products generate the highest profit?
Are discounts increasing revenue but reducing margins?
Which customer segments drive the most value?
How does performance vary by region?

🛠 Tools & Technologies
SQL (Data querying & joins)
Power BI (Dashboard & data modeling)
Excel (Data preparation)

📊 Key Metrics
Total Revenue
Net Profit
Profit Margin %
Discount Rate
Regional Performance
Customer Segment Contribution

🔍 Key Insights
High-revenue products were not always the most profitable.
Aggressive discounting reduced overall margin performance.
A small percentage of customers contributed a large portion of revenue.
